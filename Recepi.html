Recipe of Maggi

  • Maggi
  • Water
  • Maggi masala
  • coriander leaves
  1. Take 250 ml water for one small pack of maggi
  2. Boil the water for few minutes
  3. break the maggi noodles if u want
  4. Add the maggi noodles to the boiling water
  5. Add masala to the maggi after few minutes
  6. Garnish with the coriander leaves
  7. Tasty maggi ready to eat now!!
